## üìñ About The Project

**Spooky Maze** is an AI-powered, web-based, point-and-click puzzle horror game. We challenged ourselves to create a heart-pounding psychological experience using minimal design and eerie ambiance, all within a browser. The game tests a simple question: **Can logic survive panic?**

You are an urban explorer trapped in the study of a vengeful alchemist, Master Valerius. With only one hour until the moon is at its zenith, you must solve his magnum opus and brew the "Elixir of Release." As time ticks by, his spiritual presence grows stronger, and the room itself turns against you. Fail, and your soul becomes the final ingredient.

## ‚öôÔ∏è Gameplay & Features

* **Single-Screen Point-and-Click:** The entire game unfolds in a single, detailed 2D illustration of the alchemist's study.
* **Interactive Hotspots:** Click on objects to inspect them, add them to your inventory, or solve a puzzle.
* **Inventory System:** A simple on-screen bar holds the items you collect. Use items on objects in the room to progress.
* **The "Presence" Meter:** A vial of red liquid that fills over time or with incorrect puzzle attempts. As it fills, the environment becomes more hostile with visual and audio disturbances.
* **Three Core Puzzles:**
    1.  **The Celestial Orrery:** Align the planets to unlock a hidden drawer.
    2.  **The Weighted Books:** Balance the scales with the correct alchemical tomes to find a secret compartment.
    3.  **The Potion of Sight:** Use clues from around the room to brew the final elixir in the correct sequence.
